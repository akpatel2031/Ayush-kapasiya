Our Services

Property Consultant

We are synonymous with the property market in India, helping buyers & investors, search, identify and find their homes. We are a team of professional advisors, who help buyers with the deep market knowledge and assist them throughout the buying process.

Exclusive Mandate

We ink exclusive mandate with the developers for select properties. This helps us in quickly turn around the inventory and find the best pool of buyers & investors for the property without incurring cost overruns. This also enables developers to get access to our top-notch marketing and operational know-how and maintain a single point of contact for all business needs.

Corporate Space Leasing

We work with corporates, business owners, and start-ups to help them identify prime office & commercial spaces to lease-out or buy. Our core office leasing expertise is further augmented with feasibility analysis, asset management, and funding know-how, which enables us to guide our clients through every stage of the property cycle.

Assured Rental Services

We offer risk-mitigated higher ROI assured rental properties, cutting across diverse assets and market types. Our assured rental properties are mostly situated in high-growth markets with strong inherent demand, which naturally contains risk and results in elevated returns.

Pre-Leased Properties

We offer a basket of pre-leased commercial assets to our clients to invest and reap higher benefits. Investing in pre-leased properties is an attractive option for making stable rental income and earning higher capital appreciation in the mid-to-long run. In the foreseeable future, it will be amongst the most desirable investment options.

Commercial Land selling

We work closely with commercial real estate developers and industries to find apt land parcels at the locations of their choice. We have dedicated teams to simultaneously take care of financial, legal, and technical requirements.
